💡 avoir à + infinitive = another way to say "to have to / must." Slightly less forceful than devoir. J'ai à faire = I have things to do. Ce que vous avez à faire = what you have to do. Also note the imperative: Faites ! = Do it! Imperatives drop the subject and use the verb directly — Partez = Leave. Venez = Come.
